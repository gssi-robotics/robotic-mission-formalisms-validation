Survey on Mission Specification and Execution Formalisms

Introduction

We are a research group studying modeling formalisms for robotic mission specification and execution, including Behavior Trees (BTs), State Machines (SMs), Hierarchical Task Networks (HTNs), and Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN).

The purpose of this study is to gather expert insights regarding the strengths, limitations, and expected usage patterns of these formalisms. Your responses will support a comparative analysis of their expressive power, practical adoption, and suitability for different mission types and contexts.

Your data will be processed anonymously, stored securely, and used exclusively for academic research. Only the research team will have access to the raw data. You may withdraw from participation at any time by closing the survey before submission.

If you have questions or ethical concerns, please contact: research@domain.com.
