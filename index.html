Survey on Formalisms for Robotic Mission Specification & Execution

Introduction

We are a research group investigating modelling formalisms for robotic mission specification and execution. The goal of this study is to collect expert feedback on widely adopted formalisms (BTs, SMs, HTNs, BPMN) and evaluate their expressiveness, practical usage, tool support, and limitations.

Your data will be processed anonymously. Only aggregated results will be published. Only the research team will have access to the raw data. You may withdraw at any moment by closing the survey before submitting.

For questions or ethical concerns, contact: research@domain.com.
